About

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Departmenf of Physics at Harvard University. My research focuses on Beyond the Standard Model Physics, involving strongly coupled scenarios and their extra-dimensional duals using AdS/CFT. My present focus is on early Universe cosmology. I am additionally interested in applications of AI methods in high energy physics.

Before this, I was an INFN fellow, joint at Scuola Normale Superiore (SNS) Pisa and INFN Pisa, Italy. I completed my PhD in high energy Physics at University of Maryland, College Park.
